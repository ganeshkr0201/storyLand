The Wizard's Apprentice

6-8 years Intermediate Fantasy

In a hidden valley surrounded by misty mountains stood the most magical school in the world - Starfall Academy.

This was where young wizards and witches came to learn the art of magic. Among them was a young boy named Leo, who had just received his first wand.

On his first day, Leo met his classmates: Emma, who could make flowers bloom with a wave of her hand; Max, who could turn invisible when he was nervous; and Lily, who could talk to animals. Their teacher, Professor Moonbeam, was a kind but strict wizard with a long silver beard that sparkled like stars.

Their first lesson was in Basic Spells. "Magic," said Professor Moonbeam, "is not just about waving wands and saying words. It's about believing in yourself and understanding the world around you." He showed them how to make a feather float, and soon the classroom was filled with floating feathers of all colors.
