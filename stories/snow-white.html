Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs

5-10 years intermediate fairy tale
Once upon a time, there was a beautiful princess named Snow White. Her stepmother, the Queen, was very vain and jealous of Snow White's beauty.
The Queen had a magic mirror that she would ask, "Mirror, mirror on the wall, who is the fairest of them all?" The mirror always replied, "You are the fairest, my Queen." But one day, it said, "Snow White is the fairest of them all."
The Queen was furious! She ordered a huntsman to take Snow White into the forest and kill her. But the huntsman couldn't do it. He let Snow White go and told her to run away and never return.
Snow White ran deep into the forest until she found a small cottage. Inside, everything was tiny - tiny chairs, tiny beds, tiny plates. The cottage belonged to seven dwarfs who worked in a nearby mine.
When the dwarfs came home, they were surprised to find Snow White asleep in their beds. She woke up and told them her story. The dwarfs felt sorry for her and invited her to live with them.
The Queen discovered that Snow White was still alive. Disguised as an old woman, she brought Snow White a poisoned apple. When Snow White took a bite, she fell into a deep sleep.
The dwarfs placed Snow White in a glass coffin in the forest. One day, a prince found her and was amazed by her beauty. He kissed her, breaking the spell, and Snow White awoke.
Snow White and the Prince were married in a grand ceremony. They lived happily ever after, while the wicked Queen was never heard from again.
